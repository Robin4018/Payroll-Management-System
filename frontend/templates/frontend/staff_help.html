Frequently Asked Questions

When will my payslip be generated?
Payslips are typically generated within the first 3-5 working days of the following month after payroll processing is finalized by the administration.
How do I claim a reimbursement?
You can submit a new expense claim along with the supporting receipts through the "Reimbursements" module from your dashboard.
What should I do if my attendance is incorrect?
Please raise an attendance regularisation request or contact your department admin with your biometric logs for manual verification.
